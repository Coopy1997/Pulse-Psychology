## Project Overview

Renueral Psy is a custom-built psychology website and practice administration platform. It combines a calm, professional public website with an interactive booking journey and a structured administrative workspace.

The experience is designed to help clients quickly understand the practice, explore appropriate therapy pathways, review fees and funding information, and book without navigating a long or confusing website.

## Client Experience

- Clear overview of the practice and available therapy pathways
- Interactive service selection with concise clinical information
- Transparent consultation fees, funding options and rebate guidance
- In-person and telehealth consultation choices
- Guided booking intake with referral and consent handling
- Live Halaxy appointment calendar and payment widget
- Responsive experience across desktop, tablet and mobile
- Accessible crisis-support information and contact pathways

## Therapy Pathways

The website currently presents the following areas of support:

- Psychological Therapy
- Trauma Therapy
- Psychedelic Integration Therapy
- Dream Analysis
- Somatic & Nervous System Focused Therapy
- Mindfulness & Clinical Hypnotherapy
